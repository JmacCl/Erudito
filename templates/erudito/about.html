FAQ

What is this website?

This is Quora/Reddit inspired web application to help high school students during COVID find resources for online learning, with a forum page to ask questions on subjects.

What are some of the features of this website?

Features of the website include categories to contain subjects. Discussion threads on certain topics of the subjects. Pages that can include links to other sites that may be of use to students. The ability to rate or flag answers and answerers based on their quality and appropriateness respectively. Includes an about page to include information on a guide to the website, FAQ's and contact links to ask questions/give suggestions.

Who created this website?

This website was created by University of Glasgow students: Kyle Buchanan, Michael Lordes Richards and James McClure.
